About RUDRA-SETU

RUDRA-SETU is designed to map NCC cadets' academic specializations to community roles — combining discipline, training and verified status to offer ethical, accountable public service.

Core:
Scalable • Ethical • Accountable • Community-first
Pilot:
Region-focused rollout with rigorous documentation and evaluation.

Cadets act as facilitators of information and immediate support, with clear escalation to professional services when required.

Values
  • Protection & trained action (Rudra)
  • Bridging communities and institutions (Setu)
  • Students as facilitators — not authorities
  • Transparency, ethics & respect for privacy
Ethics & Safety

Mandatory ethical training, clear referral pathways, and multi-tiered monitoring ensure safety and accountability.

Implementation Roadmap

1 — Registration

Skill & availability mapping on a digital platform.

Cadets complete a verification profile.

2 — Micro-Training

Standardized modules + practical simulations.

Short modular courses delivered by experts.

3 — Service Hours

Minimum hours + flexible schedule integration.

Cadets commit to a minimum service threshold.

4 — Monitoring

On-ground supervisors + dashboard reporting.

Multi-tier monitoring with dashboards.

5 — Recognition

Certificates, credits, awards.

Certificates and awards for cadets.

6 — Pilot & Scale

Phased expansion with documentation.

Start in a defined pilot region.
